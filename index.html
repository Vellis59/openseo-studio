Generate an SEO article

Enter your keyword, choose style and language, and let your model do the work.

How it works

  1. Open the settings menu and paste your OpenRouter API key.
  2. Select your preferred model.
  3. Enter a keyword and configure tone, length, and language.
  4. Click “Generate article” and wait for the response.
  5. Copy the Markdown and drop it into your CMS, Obsidian, or static site.

Everything runs in your browser. There is no backend and no database. You are responsible for your OpenRouter usage and billing.
